英语发音练习（绕口令合集）

发表于2018-05-30

英语绕口令练习大全

💬 练习建议：从慢到快，重复多次，注意发音清晰、语调自然。适合练习连读、爆破音、齿音、卷舌音等。

🌟 经典绕口令 Top 50

  1. A big black bug bit a big black bear, made the big black bear bleed blood.
    一只大黑虫咬了一只大黑熊，让大黑熊流了血。

  2. A proper cup of coffee from a proper copper coffeepot.
    用一个正宗的铜制咖啡壶泡一杯地道的咖啡。

  3. She sells seashells by the seashore.
    她在海边卖贝壳。

  4. How can a clam cram in a clean cream can?
    一个蛤蜊怎么能塞进一个干净的奶油罐里？

  5. Red lorry, yellow lorry, red lorry, yellow lorry.
    红色卡车，黄色卡车，红色卡车，黄色卡车。

  6. Six slimy snails sailed silently.
    六只黏糊糊的蜗牛悄无声息地航行。

  7. I scream, you scream, we all scream for ice cream!
    我叫喊，你叫喊，我们都为冰淇淋尖叫！

  8. Fuzzy Wuzzy was a bear. Fuzzy Wuzzy had no hair. Fuzzy Wuzzy wasn’t fuzzy, was he?
    毛茸茸的福兹·瓦兹是只熊。但他没有毛。所以福兹·瓦兹不“毛茸茸”，对吧？

  9. Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers. A peck of pickled peppers Peter Piper picked.
    彼得·派珀采了一配克腌辣椒。彼得·派珀采的是一配克腌辣椒。

  10. I saw Susie sitting in a shoeshine shop.
    我看见苏西坐在一家擦鞋店里。

  11. Sally sells seashells down by the seashore.
    莎莉在海边卖贝壳。

  12. Toy boat, toy boat, toy boat.
    玩具船，玩具船，玩具船。

  13. I’m not a pheasant plucker, I’m a pheasant plucker’s mate, and I’m only plucking pheasants ‘cause the pheasant plucker’s late.
    我不是拔松鸡毛的人，我是他的搭档，我只是在帮他拔，因为他迟到了。

  14. How much wood would a woodchuck chuck, if a woodchuck could chuck wood?
    如果土拨鼠能扔木头，它能扔多少木头？

  15. A skunk sat on a stump and thunk the stump stunk, but the stump thunk the skunk stunk.
    一只臭鼬坐在树桩上，觉得树桩发臭；但树桩觉得是臭鼬才臭。

  16. I have got a date at a quarter to eight. I’ll see you at the gate, so don’t be late.
    我八点差一刻有个约会。我会在门口等你，别迟到。

  17. She stood on the balcony, inexplicably mimicking him, hiccuping, and amicably welcoming him in.
    她站在阳台上，莫名其妙地模仿他，打着嗝，还友好地请他进来。

  18. Betty Botter bought some butter, but she said the butter’s bitter. If I put it in my batter, it will make my batter bitter. So she bought some better butter, better than the bitter butter, and she put it in her batter, and the batter was not bitter. So it was better Betty Botter bought some better butter.
    贝蒂·波特买了黄油，但她说黄油发苦。如果放进面糊，面糊也会变苦。于是她买了更好的黄油，比苦黄油好。她把新黄油放进面糊，面糊就不苦了。所以贝蒂买更好的黄油是明智的。

  19. Pad kid poured curd pulled cod.
    皮球小孩倒了凝乳，扯出了一条鳕鱼。

  20. How much ground would a groundhog hog, if a groundhog could hog ground?
    如果土拨鼠能霸占土地，它会霸占多少？

  21. Moses supposes his toeses are roses, but Moses supposes erroneously.
    摩西以为他的脚趾是玫瑰，但他搞错了。

  22. How much dough would a doughnut make, if a doughnut could make dough?
    如果甜甜圈能做面团，它能做出多少面团？

  23. She saw Sherry, she saw Sherry’s shoes, but she saw Sherry’s shoes on the sofa. Which one of the three Saws saw Sherry’s shoes on the sofa?
    她看见了雪莉，看见了雪莉的鞋，而且鞋在沙发上。这三个“Saw”中，是谁看见鞋在沙发上的？

  24. I thought I thought of thinking of thanking you.
    我以为我想到过要感谢你。

  25. A cup of proper coffee in a copper coffee cup.
    一杯地道的咖啡，盛在铜制咖啡杯里。

  26. How can you convince me to conceive this conceit that I’m conceived to conceive?
    你怎么能说服我接受这种“我生来就该有野心”的自负想法？

  27. Can you can a can as a canner can can a can?
    你能像罐头工一样把罐头装进罐头里吗？

  28. I saw a saw that could outsaw any other saw I ever saw.
    我见过一把锯子，它能比任何我见过的锯子都锯得快。

  29. Wayne went to Wales to watch walruses.
    韦恩去威尔士看海象。

  30. Betty and Bob brought back blue balloons from the big bazaar.
    贝蒂和鲍勃从大集市带回了蓝色气球。

  31. How many yaks could a yak pack pack, if a yak pack could pack yaks?
    如果一个牦牛驮包能装牦牛，那它能装多少头牦牛？

  32. A sailor went to sea to see, what he could see. And all he could see was sea, sea, sea.
    一名水手去海上看风景，他所能看到的只有海，海，海。

  33. I wish to wish the wish you wish to wish, but if you wish the wish the witch wishes, I won’t wish the wish you wish to wish.
    我想许下你想许的愿望，但如果那是女巫的愿望，我就不愿许了。

  34. The sixth sick sheik’s sixth sheep’s sick.
    第六个生病的酋长的第六只羊病了。

  35. The seething sea ceaseth and thus the seething sea sufficeth us.
    翻腾的大海平息了，因此它已足够满足我们。

  36. How many berries could a bare berry carry, if a bare berry could carry berries?
    如果一朵光秃秃的浆果能携带浆果，那它能带多少？

  37. I saw a saw in Arkansas that would outsaw any saw I ever saw saw.
    我在阿肯色见到一把锯子，它比我见过的任何锯子都更锋利。

  38. I slit the sheet, the sheet I slit, and on the slitted sheet I sit.
    我撕开床单，就是我撕的那张床单，我坐在撕开的床单上。

  39. Can you imagine an imaginary menagerie manager, imagining managing an imaginary menagerie?
    你能想象一个虚构的动物园管理员，正在想象管理一个虚构的动物园吗？

  40. If you must cross a course cross cow across a crowded cow crossing, cross the cross coarse cow across the crowded cow crossing carefully.
    如果你必须赶一头倔强的母牛穿过拥挤的牛道，请小心地赶过去。

  41. Luke’s duck likes lakes. Luke Luck licks lakes. Luke’s duck licks lakes. Ducks like Luke Luck’s lakes. Luke Luck licks lakes. Luck’s duck licks lakes. Who’s lakes does Luke Luck’s duck like?
    卢克的鸭子喜欢湖。卢克·拉克舔湖。卢克的鸭子也舔湖。鸭子喜欢卢克·拉克的湖。那么，卢克·拉克的鸭子喜欢谁的湖？

  42. A rough-coated, dough-faced, thoughtful ploughman strode through the streets of Scarborough; after falling into a slough, he coughed and hiccoughed.
    一位皮糙脸圆、沉思的农夫走过斯卡伯勒的街道；掉进泥沼后，他咳嗽并打嗝。

  43. Double bubble gum, bubbles double.
    双倍泡泡糖，吹出双倍泡泡。

  44. If Stu chews shoes, should Stu choose the shoes he chews?
    如果斯图爱嚼鞋子，他该不该挑选要嚼的鞋？

  45. Friendly fleas and fireflies, flitting and flying over fur and foliage, flutes and fiddles in the fields that they fill with their fervor.
    友好的跳蚤和萤火虫，在毛皮与树叶间飞舞，用热情填满了田野里的长笛与小提琴。

  46. Moses supposes his toeses are roses, but Moses supposes erroneously, for Moses he knowses his toeses aren’t roses as Moses supposes his toeses to be.
    摩西以为脚趾是玫瑰，但他错了，因为摩西知道他的脚趾根本不是玫瑰。

  47. She sells seashells on the seashore. The shells she sells are seashells, I’m sure.
    她在海滩上卖贝壳。她卖的贝壳当然是海贝壳。

  48. Amidst the mists and coldest frosts, with stoutest wrists and loudest boasts, he thrusts his fist against the posts and still insists he sees the ghosts.
    在浓雾与严霜中，他用最强壮的手腕和最响亮的吹嘘，把拳头砸向柱子，还坚称看到了鬼魂。

  49. I have a tongue-twister, a lovely tongue-twister, and it is this: unique New York.
    我有个绕口令，一个可爱的绕口令：独特的纽约（Unique New York）。

  50. Summer sunshine, summer sky, summer yellow, summer high.
    夏日阳光，夏日天空，夏日金黄，夏日高涨。

  51. A clumsy bee that bumps into trees makes valleys ring with hums and sees.
    一只笨拙的蜜蜂撞上树，山谷回荡着嗡嗡声和“see”音（双关：see /siː/ 与 hum）。

  52. In Hertford, Hereford and Hampshire, hurricanes hardly ever happen.
    在赫特福德、赫里福德和汉普郡，飓风几乎从不发生。

  53. A noise annoys an oyster, but a noisy noise annoys an oyster more.
    声音惹恼牡蛎，但吵闹的声音更惹恼牡蛎。
