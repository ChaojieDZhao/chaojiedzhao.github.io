发表于2018-05-10

英语发音规则

音节基础 Basics of Syllable

  • ^Decade ^college ^female ja^pan

动词和名词的重读 Syllables of Verbs and Nouns

简而言之，单词在做名词和做动词时的重读音节不一样。
noun = stress first syllable
verb = stress second syllable

  • I hope to proGRESS.
  • You are making good PROgress.

“Ate”结尾的单词 Ate Words

简而言之，做动词时发作/eit/，做名词或者做形容词时发作/ɪt/

  • Graduate
  • Estimate
  • Articulate
  • Did he graduate from college?
  • Yes and now he’s in graduate school working on his PhD.
  • I will calculate it and give you an estimate.
  • I really appreciate it.

后缀的强读 Stress Rules for Suffixes

  • -ese需要强读 Chinese Japanese Portuguese
  • -eer需要强读 pioneer volunteer engineer
  • -ique需要强度 antique critique technique
  • -ic 强读该后缀之前的音节 realistic Electric strategic optimistic traffic
  • 名词到形容词重音节的转换（重音后移）。
    • Origin original
    • Industry industrial
    • Memory memorial
    • Finance financial
    • President Presidential

How Unstressed Syllables Work

简而言之，除了重读音节的元音字母，其他的都转化为/ə/

  • Madam problem symptom album o’clock
  • Adam=atom

句子中单词的强读 Basics of Stressing the Right Words Within Sentences （更大声更高调）

Content words: 富有最关键信息的单词，需要强读。
Function words: 不太关键的单词，则不需要。

  • I will be driving to the market to buy something to eat.

Second content word gets more stress.

  • I got a new job
  • Do me a favor.
  • See you later.
  • Let’s go for a walk.
  • Did you get it. 这个不是it，因为他是代词。
  • Did you see the film.
  • Who stole it?
  • Who stole my money?
  • Who stole the money from me?
  • I was washing the dishes for her.
  • I couldn’t find one for a long time.

核心词和意组 Focus Words and Thought Groups

简而言之，长句中需要切割成thought groups.
每一个thought group都有Focus words.

  • I was watching a movie With my friends when suddenly it starts to rain.
  • I was so tired and I couldn’t wait to get home.

Compound Nouns

简而言之，强读复合名词的第一个单词。并且两个单词，一定要连续。

  • Credit card
  • Eyebrow Forehead
  • eyelet hairstyle hairsalon
  • I got a headache from the loud rock music.

Phrasal Verbs

简而言之，强读短语动词的第二个单词。

  • pick up
  • break down

短语动词做名词的时候还是要强读第一个单词。

  • I had a great workout.
  • I found out about it.
  • Can you look it over for me?
  • I will think it over and I will call you back.

Names of Places and People.

  • South America
  • United Kingdom.
  • Los Angeles.
  • Las Vegas.
    如果是人名的话，就选最后一个。

Abbreviations and Numbers

缩写时一定要连续，最后一个字母强读。

  • CNN
  • Phd

数字也是最后一个字母强读。

Stress for Special Emphasis

简而言之，你想强调哪部分，就重读那部分

  • -You don’t smoke do you? -I used to smoke.

Rising and Falling Intonation

Stress and intonation，stress是在哪里强读，而intonation是如何去stress。即可以在某个强读的单词去升或者降。

  • all right ⬇️
  • all right？⬆️
  • hello？⬆️
  • yes/no questions使用升
  • why questions使用降

Changing Information

在一个系列中，前面的升最后一个降。升调表示没有说完。

  • I brought chicken fish apples and Sugar⬇ at the supermarket. Sugar降，其他升。
